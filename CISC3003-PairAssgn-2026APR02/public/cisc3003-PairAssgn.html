About Us

NovaPair Web Studio is a collaborative web design initiative established by Yiming Luo and Hao Yang. Throughout our study of web development and computer technologies, we gradually came to recognize that a well-designed website requires not only technical implementation, but also thoughtful information architecture, clear visual hierarchy, and a user-oriented design perspective.

Through this collaboration, we aim to build our own business website and demonstrate our capabilities in web design, front-end development, and professional growth. Our work is intended for personal branding, portfolio presentation, and small-scale business promotion.

  • Responsive web design
  • Portfolio production
  • Showcase-style business sites
  • Content-focused structure planning

Meet The Partners

Concise Partner Introductions

Yiming Luo

Yiming Luo is a Computer Science student with a solid programming foundation and strong abilities in information organization. Through academic study and project-based training, he has developed a sustained interest in webpage structure design, content planning, and user experience presentation.

In this collaboration, he is primarily responsible for overall content planning, page structure design, layout organization, and professional presentation, with the goal of integrating technical competence and personal development through web-based expression.

Personal Portfolio Business Qualification

Hao Yang

Hao Yang is a fellow Computer Science student and a classmate of Yiming Luo, with a strong interest in web development and digital expression. He pays close attention to interface cleanliness, the usability of navigation design, and the intuitive presentation of page content.

In this collaboration, he mainly contributes to content organization, interface refinement, and overall project coordination, and aims to further strengthen his front-end development skills and teamwork experience through this website design practice.
